Progress Report in Jan, 2023

Posted on 2023-01-01 Edited on 2023-05-14 In BiWeeklyUpdate

“Our destiny is written in the stars.”

It’s the last day of 2022, 7:45 pm. Let’s summarize the past year.

Plans

I had a lot of plans in my head. Every plan felt like a commander’s order that tortured the real me to death.

“I need to finish reading this book during the holiday.” Often, I couldn’t finish it, and the progress was slow. I tortured myself.

But this year is different. First of all, I let myself go, and I became a lazy bum. What can you do to me? Second, I think I found the right direction. The previous plans always listed things that I resisted. I didn’t want to face reality, so I put them in the plan and gave them to my future self to solve and face.

This holiday, I executed the plan quite well, even exceeding expectations. The reason is that I really want to understand some ethereum details. I am quite satisfied. I hope to make a good start.

Diary

I have been writing a diary since I was six years old, and I have been writing for twenty-four years. Sometimes I would reflect on whether I over-reflect, causing me to hesitate when making big decisions and being indecisive.

Later, I found out that it was not the case. I knew in my heart when it was time to make a big decision, but it was difficult for me to describe the time node. I just knew that it was time to leave and it was time to set off. You don’t have to ask me why. If I have to use a metaphor, it’s like a monster in your heart that keeps telling you to change, and it’s time to do it. I have to respect this monster.

Currently, I am quite satisfied with the big decisions in my life. If I am not satisfied, it means that it is not yet time for the decision to reach its conclusion.

“As for life-changing decisions, you must take risks. Significant things always happen by coincidence.”

Age

It’s too hard to imagine. When I was eighteen or nineteen, I thought thirty was far away. I thought thirty was completely middle-aged. What should I do? I thought I would have a net worth of over 100 million yuan when I turned thirty. Haven’t even crossed ten million yet? Then I can go to hell. That’s what I really thought when I was nineteen.

But when I really reached this age, the reality is that I am still worrying about the next meal, still being criticized by my boss, still anxious and unhappy.

Daily Work

I saw an interview where Lu Yu said that she had been doing “Lu Yu You Yue” for twenty years, and her feeling was that “she occasionally gets tired but never annoyed.” When I saw this sentence, I felt I am the opposite. “I am not very tired, but I am annoyed.”

This year, work is still a troublesome thing. Next year, I want to achieve not being too influenced by work on my personal life. Take it easy. Work is not that important. Is others’ opinion important? Thicken my skin a bit. Others don’t care that much.

Experience and Creation

Travel more and experience different lives.

This year, I feel my energy decreasing and need a catalyst, like coffee, to forcibly stay alert and face the chaos of life.

Should I still aim higher? Of course, I should still aim higher.

Failure, or being “failed”, is normal, but even in any desperate situation, one should not give up their only one life.

I want to watch movies, work out, scuba dive, surf, write novels, paint, play games, and write games.

I want freedom.

I like math and beauty. I like comics, detective novels, and imagination.

I like and appreciate creativity.

I hate repetition and being commanded.

I hate hopelessness.

“This is a world of many regrets and beauty.” Yes.

Still run towards the direction of “freely and unrestrainedly wandering”.

At the end of 2022, I am grateful that the fire in my heart is still burning and there is still fuel. I still hold onto my ideals.

“Freely and unrestrainedly wandering.” The picture these four words create in my mind is this: it’s snowing heavily outside, but it’s very, very warm inside the house, always warm. There is a lot of food, always enough. I just lie on the couch, covered with a thin blanket, eating snacks and watching movies. It snows for a day, two days, and even if it snows for a year, I don’t care, I only care about snacks and fire. Whether there is enough money or not, I don’t care, I only care about snacks and fire. When I reach a certain age, whether I get married and have children or not, I don’t care. I just stay in my house like this, one day, two days, until I die.

The ideal I want to achieve is to be able to do what I like in my house, without being bound by others, and the gaze of others cannot hurt me. I am free and unrestrained, wandering. Watching the sun rise and fall, snowflakes fluttering, I still only care about my snacks and fire.

How are you doing now? Are you free? In 2018, I felt like I was still a long way off, but in 2022, I would say that I’m only three thousand miles away.

God exists, and I firmly believe this. He will lend you a hand when things are out of control. There is always a way out. But when he stretches out his hand, you have to grab it and not let this guy run away.

2023 will definitely get better. Something big, something big will happen.

Let’s go, 2023.
